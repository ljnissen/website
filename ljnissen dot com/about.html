Thanks for visiting my site. I'm LJ Nissen.

I'm an investor, author and writer of software. In my forthcoming book The Junior Mining Valuation Handbook, I will lay out a stepwise method of valuing junior resource projects. I also take prudent interest in technical analysis - an enormously powerful tool to determine the ups and downs of the stockmarket.

Coquilles is my software company where I release my apps and do consulting work. Contact me there if you have a project that you want me to help you with.

Outside of work I enjoy traveling and acting on stage. I've been a member of local theater classes for almost fifteen years now.
