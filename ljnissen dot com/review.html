Predictions 2015

As 2015 draws to a close I will huddle up in my study, dim the lights and take a deep look in the crystal ball to predict what is going to happen in 2015.

First of all, the oil price. It is fair to assume that the collapse in oil prices has gone a little bit to far and that prices will recover in the new year.

The question is how much.

Taking a look at the fundamentals it is clear to see that the lower prices are now taking their toll on the high cost US shale oil and the lower the prices go the more companies will be affected.

And the higher they will rebound.

What about the the precious metals?

The gold price has had a rocky year, and as I write this in the final hours of 2014, it does not appear to close above the 1200 dollar mark.

These days what is fundamentally driving the price is deflation, or rather, the expectation of deflation. This will probably shift more towards inflation next year as the US economy picks up speed.

I believe that the huge monetary experiment run by the Federal Reserve will finally come home to roost in 2015 and that we will see exploding prices in all precious metals.

At the same time the gold stocks are near record lows and just waiting for some action in the gold price to have a huge move.

So now is the time to back up the truck.
